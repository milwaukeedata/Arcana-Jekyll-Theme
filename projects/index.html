MKE 2016 Open Data Survey

The Milwaukee Community Database is a project of Milwaukee Neighborhood News Service.We are interested in providing information to the City of Milwaukee about what data residents want to be able to access. Please reply to this short survey, and we will compile the results and deliver them to the mayor's office, the city's Chief Information Officer and each alderperson. The information provided will be aggregated and anonymous. Want to be an identified voice? Contact us at contact@milwaukeedata.org

MKe Digital Public Works

The Milwaukee Digital Public Works is a community of Milwaukee residents who want to improve the city by designing and building technology services. We accept submissions of unsolved problems in the city and crowdsource solutions to them. Once a solution has been identified, we help collect the resources necessary to execute the solution and coordinate the team who does the fixing.

Project teams are made up of people with diverse expertise and backgrounds to make sure that a true solution is created. This means getting programmers, designers, city employees, and residents to all work together on the problem.

Milwaukee Police Department Dispatch Logs

This project is an effort to provide a more useful view of the Milwaukee Police Department (MPD) call dispatch log, which is provided by the MPD here. Their live feed provides the most recent 90 minutes of calls dispatched. This dataset attempts to catalog the entire history, going back to October 19, 2016 (the start of this project). If you would like to contribute to this project, or request changes, please email nick@rokkincat.com. The hosting and development of this project has been donated by the local software contracting firm RokkinCat.
